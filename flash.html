Flash XRP Firmware

Use this page to manually flash firmware to your XRP when it's unresponsive or needs recovery.

Step 1: Enter Bootloader Mode

Follow these steps to put your XRP into bootloader mode:

1

Plug in USB

Connect your XRP to your computer using a USB cable.

2

Hold down BOOT

Press and hold the BOOT button on your XRP.

3

Hold down RESET

While still holding BOOT, press and hold the RESET button.

4

Release RESET

Let go of the RESET button while keeping BOOT held down.

5

Release BOOT

Let go of the BOOT button. Your XRP should now appear as a USB drive (RPI-RP2 or RP2350).

Step 2: Flash Firmware

Click the button below and select the mounted RPI-RP2 or RP2350 drive.

Step 3: Return to Editor

After flashing completes, your XRP will automatically reboot. Click below to return to the code editor.
